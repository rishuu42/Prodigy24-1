About Us

When human intellect and technology unite in NEXUS, they triumph over complexities and champion sustainability. It's not just a theme for Prodigy'24; it's a grand unison of humans and technology, a celebration of intellect and innovation solving complex problems.

NEXUS showcases the synergy of humans and technology for sustainability. The saying 'None of us is as smart as all of us' encapsulates the ethos of NEXUS, embracing the fusion of human brilliance and technology for common goals and sustainable solutions. But how does this fusion work? How can the blend of human prowess and technology help solve complex problems and sustain our world?

Join us on this extraordinary journey at Prodigy'24 and discover the boundless potential of Nexus. Are you ready to embrace the challenge and become a catalyst for positive change?

Events

Code Nexus

Attention coders! Dive into Code Nexus, a five-hour online competition where you'll face increasingly difficult coding problems throughout the day. The best part? You can code in any language you're comfortable with! Think fast, write accurately, and show off your coding skills to conquer each challenge and win your share of a massive ₹18,000 prize pool!

Bits and Bids

Bits and Bids, a coding competition brought to you by UPES ACM and ACM-W Student Chapters, throws down the gauntlet. Sharpen your skills with a challenging quiz that unlocks bidding power for the main event: an epic coding showdown! Here, you'll strategically use points earned in the quiz to bid on various coding challenges, putting your skills to the test and vying for awesome prizes. Don't miss this chance to prove your coding prowess and claim victory!

Unearth Utopia

Ready to unleash your inner explorer? Join the adventure of a lifetime at Prodigy'24, presented by UPES ACM and ACM-W Student Chapters! Dive into the thrill of Unearth Utopia, our exhilarating treasure hunt event showcasing sustainability and nature's beauty on campus. With prizes worth 18000 up for grabs, gather your squad of 3 or 4 and set forth on an unforgettable journey. Don't miss out! Register now and let the exploration begin!

LensLore

Embark on an enchanting journey with Lenslore! This exciting event will unfold across three dynamic rounds. In Round 1, participants will unleash their creativity by capturing the blend of technology, humanity, and nature in a single photograph. Progressing to Round 2, participants will dive into a storytelling adventure with a captivating series of three photographs. Round 3 will bring it all together, challenging participants to showcase real-world solutions through compelling videos. Throughout this experience, your creativity and originality will be the key factors judged. Join us as the lens unveils a captivating world where technology, humanity, and nature converge in harmony.

Vaad Vivaad 2.0

Calling all brainiacs! Join Vaad-Vivaad 2.0 by UPES ACM and ACM-W Student Chapters. Collaborate in group discussions, battle it out in fiery debates, and unleash your wit in the extempore round. This is your chance to showcase your thinking skills, teamwork, and spontaneous brilliance - don't miss out!

Metamorphosis

Attention artists! Calling all creative minds to Metamorphosis, a graphic story competition by UPES ACM and ACM-W Student Chapters. This is your chance to unleash your inner artist and transform your ideas into stunning graphic stories. Push your artistic boundaries - create bold visuals and captivating narratives to compete for the title of ultimate masterpiece! Sharpen your pencils, hone your skills, and get ready to showcase your creative genius - this artistic journey awaits!

ENVIROPRENEUR

Got Eco-Brains? Prove It! UPES ACM & ACM-W Student Chapters challenge you to "ENVIROPRENEUR"! Gear up for a Green Trivia Blitz, testing eco-startup know-how. Then, solve real environmental puzzles, crafting game-changing solutions. Impress with presentations or prototypes! Win ₹15,000 & become a green tech pioneer. ENVIROPRENEUR isn't just a competition; it's your springboard to a greener future.

DSP
